Our Story

Named with love,
built on experience

Aubrey Watson is named after our founder's grandfather, Herbert Aubrey Watson, whose love of gardening and nature inspires the calm, natural feel you'll find throughout our funeral home.

With years of experience caring for families across South East Wales, we opened our doors in Blackwood to offer something different — unhurried, honest, personal support without pressure or fuss.

We're a qualified embalmer and proud member of the British Institute of Embalmers, committed to the highest standards of care for your loved one.

THE STORY

Dai Evans opened this shop in '87 with one chair, a set of clippers, and a radio tuned to Radio Wales. His son Gethin took over in 2008. Now Gethin's boy Rhys is on the second chair. Three generations, same standards — a proper cut, a decent conversation, and you're out the door looking tidy.

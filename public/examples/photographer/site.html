About

I'm Huw — based in Pontypridd, shooting across South Wales. I've been behind a camera since I was 14, studying photography at USW, and working professionally since 2016. I shoot weddings, portraits, landscapes, and commercial work.

My approach is simple: natural light where possible, real moments over posed ones, and images that feel like the day felt — not like a catalogue. I deliver every gallery within 14 days, fully edited, no watermarks, full print rights.

— Huw
